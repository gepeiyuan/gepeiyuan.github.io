Hello! My name is Ge Peiyuan~

The research areas I am currently engaged in include critical thinking in the GAI-assisted design process, the impact of GAI on design education, HAI, and design practice. Right now, I'm applying for a PhD in Design and looking for long-term partners. If you're interested, please get in touch with me by email.

In September 2023, I was recommended to pursue a Master's degree in Mechanical Engineering with a specialization in Innovative Design and Entrepreneurship at the College of Design and Creativity, Tongji University, without taking the entrance examination. My academic advisor is Associate Professor Fan Fei. My cumulative grade point average (GPA) is 4.31 out of 5.0. I have been awarded the National Postgraduate Scholarship and recognized as an Outstanding Student Leader at Tongji University. As of May 2025, three first-author conference papers have been published and several practical awards have been received.

I graduated from the Communication University of China as an outstanding graduate of Beijing, earning a dual bachelor's degree in Visual Communication Design and Digital Media Art under the supervision of Professor Wu Dingyu. My GPA was 3.81/4.0, first in comprehensive assessment. During my undergraduate studies, I received two national scholarships, the title of Beijing Outstanding Student Leader, and the May 4th Youth Medal from the Communication University of China.

I am also committed to independently exploring design issues on the theme of my master's graduation thesis "Innovative Design as an auxiliary tool to promote critical thinking for beginners in design". In the next few years, I hope to conduct design projects with research questions.

    • Entrepreneurial practice System design Research on design and application of Winter Olympic Cultural heritage based on artificial intelligence   [Project details]
  • This project develops a series of literary and creative works around the legacy of the Winter Olympics, mainly for the "one-click Plog" photo punch program, "Winter Dream Rabbit" IP and "Play the Winter Olympics" interactive games. "One-click Plog" is an APP that quickly satisfies users' desire to share through intelligent recognition and automatic generation. "Winter Dream Rabbit" is a new era artificial intelligence robot based on the Beijing intangible heritage Rabbit, to help tourists better experience the Olympic culture. "Playing the Winter Olympics" through the embodied theory, with the tourists "present" way to participate in the "arbitrary" scene exploration, in-depth experience of the Winter Olympics venues, Winter Olympics sports, Winter Olympics spirit, to create a sustainable development of the Winter Olympics space. This series of products is a one-stop tour of the Winter Olympic heritage ecological chain, through a convenient and efficient way to convey the Winter Olympic culture, spirit and cultural heritage to users.
  • As the founder of the project, I recruited team members, carried out brainstorming, market research, project planning, visual design, product development, and document writing. The project went through two iterations for eight months, and I was responsible for the main visual presentation, UI design, PPT production and video editing of the project, as well as the roadshow of school-level and city-level competitions.
